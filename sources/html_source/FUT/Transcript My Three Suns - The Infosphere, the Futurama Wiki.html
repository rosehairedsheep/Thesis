(17:23) ⨂Leela: [on screen] I like you.

(17:24) ⨂Bender: You do? Look, are you going to help or not?

(17:28) ⨂Leela: [on screen] I don't know why I should. I mean after what he--

(17:30) ⨂Bender: Wait, wait, wait, wait. [He crosses his legs and taps his chin.] What is it you like best about me?

[Scene: Trisol Palace Throne Room. Enter Bender.]

(17:33) ⨂Amy: Is she coming?

(17:34) ⨂Bender: I'm not sure. But I do know that she likes my in-your-face attitude.

[There is a rumbling from outside. The crew gather around a window and look out. The Trisolians pull a giant lemon juicer-like machine towards the palace.]

(17:48) ⨂Fry: What the hell is that?

(17:49) ⨂Bont: Its the Juice-A-Matic 4000. It'll strain my juices from you while filtering out the pulp. By which I mean, your shredded remains.

(17:56) ⨂Zoidberg: Of course! Why didn't I think of that!

(17:59) ⨂Fry: This is the saddest day of my life. And I still can't cry.

[Fry sits in his throne and a splashing noise from outside attracts the others to the window.]

(18:06) ⨂Bender: Wait a second. Here comes Leela.

[Cut to: Outside Trisol Palace. Leela kicks her way through the crowd of Trisolian guards, splashing them to oblivion. They swarm her.]
[Cut to: Trisol Palace Throne Room.]

(18:17) ⨂Amy: Oh, no! They have her totally outnumbered.

(18:20) ⨂Fry: I can't believe it. She's risking her life for me after the way I treated her. I don't deserve this. I feel terrible.

(18:27) ⨂Bender: You do? Hmm.

(18:30) ⨂Fry: Is she alright?

(18:31) ⨂Bender: I don't know. Perhaps I'll look out this window. Oh, dear God in heaven, they're swarming all over her.

(18:40) ⨂Fry: No. No!

(18:41) ⨂Amy: What are you talking about, Bender? She's al-- [Bender puts his hand over Amy's mouth and she eventually catches on.] (muffled) Oh!

(18:44) ⨂Bender: They're strapping her to juicer. Oh, they're putting some ice cubes in the glass under it.

(18:52) ⨂Fry: This can't be happening.

(18:53) ⨂Bender: It can and, for all you know, it is. [He puts his hand on Fry's shoulder.] I'm sorry, Fry. She's dead.

[Fry starts to cry and Amy catches his tear in the bottle.]

(19:09) ⨂Fry: All Leela ever wanted to do was help me. But I was to proud, too stupid to accept it. [He throws his crown across the room.] I wish I had died instead of her.

[He cries. Leela climbs through the window.]

(19:18) ⨂Leela: What are you talking about?

[Fry stops crying.]

(19:21) ⨂Fry: Leela! You're alive!

(19:22) ⨂Leela: Of course I'm alive.

(19:24) ⨂Bender: I told Fry you were dead so he would cry out the Emperor but you had to go and wreck it by surviving.

(19:30) ⨂Amy: We only got two drops.

[There is a bang at the door. The Trisolians begin leaking in through a hole. Bont chuckles.]

(19:37) ⨂Bont: It's only a matter of time now.

(19:39) ⨂Zoidberg: I'll handle this!

[He tries to block the leak with his claw and eventually gets it under control by forcing one of his mouth flaps into the hole.]

(19:45) ⨂Leela: Listen, Fry, I think I can get us out of this if you're willing to let me help you.

(19:48) ⨂Fry: Thanks, Leela. From now on, I'll take all the help you're willing to give. I know you just want what's best for me. [Leela smiles then stamps on his foot.] Ow! What was that for? [She slaps him.] Hey, come on! [He bursts into tears.] That hurt!

(20:03) ⨂Leela: I know. Amy, get the bottle.

[She carries on beating Fry up while Amy holds the bottle under his eye.]

(20:09) ⨂Fry: [crying] Oh, now I understand.

(20:10) ⨂Leela: Come on. Everybody help out Fry.

[She slaps him again, Zoidberg pinches his leg with his claw and Bender stubs out a cigar on his arm.]

(20:17) ⨂Fry: [crying] Thanks, everybody. I love you all. You guys are true-- Ow! Cut it out, Bender! That's a tender area!

(20:26) ⨂Leela: How we doing, Amy?

(20:27) ⨂Amy: Great! We're one-tenth of the way there.

[Time Lapse. Leela, Zoidberg and Bender are tired out. Amy continues kicking Fry, panting.]

(20:37) ⨂Amy: OK, it's your turn.

[She points to someone. A Trisolian starts to hit Fry with a chair.]

(20:40) ⨂Fry: Hey, wait a minute! Who are you?

(20:43) ⨂Bont: I'm the Emperor! Thanks for crying me out.

[Fry looks at his stomach and sees it's back to normal.]

(20:47) ⨂Fry: Oh, you're welcome.

[Bont hits him with the chair again.]
[Cut to: Outside Trisolian Palace. The crowds have gone. Fry cries in pain from inside.]

(20:55) ⨂Bender: [from inside] Hey! Save some for me!

[Closing Credits.]
