(19:12) ⨂Fry: Hey, it works! The static's gone.

(19:15) ⨂Human Friend: [on TV] I hate that this came between us, Calculon.

(19:17) ⨂Calculon: [on TV] Me too. I'm filled with a large number of powerful emotions.

(19:21) ⨂Human Friend: [on TV] You're my best friend. I'm sorry I treated you so badly.

(19:25) ⨂Calculon: [on TV] Apology accepted. After all, you're only human.

[They hug.]

(19:31) ⨂Leela: You guys could learn a lesson from those two.

(19:33) ⨂Fry: She's right.

(19:35) ⨂Bender: You're my best friend, Fry, I'm sorry I treated you so badly.

(19:40) ⨂Fry: Apology accepted. After all, you're only human.

[They hug.]

(19:45) ⨂Leela: Wait a minute! You did it all backwards. [Fry and Bender stare blankly.] Fry's the one who should be-- [Fry and Bender keep staring.] Oh, never mind!

[Fry and Bender laugh. Bender looks in the mirror and sighs.]

(19:57) ⨂Fry: What's wrong?

(19:58) ⨂Bender: Nothing. I guess I'm just going to have to get used to being half a robot.

(20:03) ⨂Fry: Aw, this isn't right. Listen, Bender, if we found your antenna could they still reattach it?

(20:08) ⨂Bender: Maybe. If we get it on ice right away.

[Scene: Outside Apartment Building. Fry, Bender, Smitty and URL search for the antenna.]

(20:13) ⨂Smitty: Bingo!

[He holds up the antenna.]

(20:15) ⨂URL: You call that an antenna?

[Scene: Robot Arms Apartments Corridor. Fry holds his miniature fruit salad tree.]

(20:22) ⨂Bender: This time, you'll have all the human comfort. We'll get a couple of toilets, some food cookers, maybe a puppy.

[Cut to: Fry's and Bender's Apartment.]

(20:33) ⨂Fry: It's good to be home.

(20:35) ⨂Bender: It sure is. By the way, I saved your stuff.

(20:38) ⨂Fry: So that's where those skin flakes went. Hmm, do you think this fruit tree's going to get enough light?

(20:44) ⨂Bender: There's a window in the closet.

[He pushes a button and a door swings open to reveal a large living space. Fry walks into it.]

(20:49) ⨂Fry: This is huge! Bender, why don't I just live in here?

(20:53) ⨂Bender: In a closet? Oh, humans!

[Closing Credits.]
